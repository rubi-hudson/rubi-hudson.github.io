Research

Incentive Structures in AI Alignment: A Game Theoretic Approach

[Abstract]
This paper explores the application of game theory to AI alignment, focusing on the design of incentive structures that promote beneficial AI behavior. We present a novel framework for modeling the interactions between AI systems and human values, and propose mechanisms for aligning AI objectives with societal goals.

The Economics of AI Safety: Balancing Innovation and Risk Mitigation

[Abstract]
This study examines the economic implications of AI safety measures, analyzing the trade-offs between rapid AI development and risk mitigation strategies. We develop a model that quantifies the potential costs and benefits of various AI safety approaches, providing insights for policymakers and researchers in the field of AI governance.
